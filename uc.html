A selection of Salesforce implementations I’ve led or contributed to as a Solution Architect / Lead, focused on multi-cloud design, automation, governance, and enterprise-scale delivery.

Enterprise Platform

Public University System – Enterprise Continuity & Operations Platform

Role: Sr Salesforce Lead / Administrator  |  Location: California  |  Timeline: 2024 – Present

Scope

  • Led Salesforce platform work for a large public university system, supporting continuity planning, IT disaster recovery, and operational readiness across multiple campuses.
  • Owned end-to-end configuration of core objects, page layouts, record types, validation rules, and automation for thousands of distributed users.
  • Worked closely with emergency management, business continuity, and IT stakeholders to align the platform with real-world processes.

Key Contributions

  • Designed and maintained complex workflow rules, approval processes, and record-triggered Flows to reduce manual effort and improve data quality.
  • Managed user access at scale: profiles, permission sets, roles, and sharing models that respect campus, department, and role-based boundaries.
  • Supported a vendor-based continuity platform (UC Ready / Fusion Risk Management) by defining requirements, testing enhancements, and coordinating releases with campus leaders.
  • Maintained administrator documentation and runbooks, ensuring changes are well-governed and repeatable across the system.

CPQ & Lead-to-Cash

T-Mobile – CPQ Lead-to-Cash Transformation

Role: Sr Salesforce Developer / Admin (Sales, Service & CPQ)  |  Locations: Dallas, TX & Seattle, WA (Remote)  |  Timeline: 2020 – 2022

Scope

  • Supported and enhanced a complex quote-to-cash stack spanning Eloqua, Salesforce, SteelBrick CPQ, Zuora, and NetSuite.
  • Worked across Opportunities, Products, Quotes, Orders, and Contracts to handle enterprise-grade lead-to-cash processes.
  • Focused on advanced pricing, discounting, approvals, and automation to support large, configurable deals.

Key Contributions

  • Configured and extended SteelBrick CPQ: product bundles, product rules, price rules, discounting strategies, and approval rules aligned with business policy.
  • Implemented bundles with complex option-level pricing, markup, and discount logic to meet specific business requirements.
  • Integrated CPQ outputs with a subscription billing system (Zuora) and downstream ERP (NetSuite) as part of an end-to-end lead-to-cash solution.
