About

A Salesforce Solution Architect and Technical Lead with over a decade of experience in software development and on the Salesforce platform, owning the full delivery lifecycle—from requirements and solution design through configuration, custom development, testing, deployment, and long-term support. I work across multiple Salesforce clouds (Sales, Service, Experience, Marketing, CPQ, and analytics), combining low-code automation with Apex, Lightning, integrations, and Agentforce AI to build scalable, secure, and human-centered solutions.

I have a strong focus on Agentforce AI–driven experiences—using predictive insights, recommendations, and intelligent automation to simplify work for users and improve decision-making. I’m comfortable leading cross-functional teams, partnering with business stakeholders, BAs, and engineers to turn complex requirements into clear architectures, reliable implementations, and well-documented platforms that continue to evolve with the organization.

Long term, I’m building toward becoming a Salesforce MVP by contributing back to the ecosystem through learning resources, knowledge sharing, and real-world patterns from complex, mission-critical implementations.

UC Ready, Business Continuity & ITDR

A core part of my work is at the intersection of Business Continuity, IT Disaster Recovery, and Emergency Management for large public institutions. I help design and evolve the UC Ready platform and related processes so that universities can understand their critical services, dependencies, and recovery strategies.

My focus here is on:

  • Translating continuity and ITDR requirements into practical Salesforce-based workflows and data models
  • Bringing together business, IT, and emergency management teams on a shared, living platform
  • Creating readiness metrics, dashboards, and processes that support decision-making before, during, and after disruptive events
