AI, Analytics & Intelligence

UC Ready, Business Continuity & ITDR

A major part of my work is at the intersection of Business Continuity, IT Disaster Recovery, and Emergency Management for large public institutions. I help design and evolve the UC Ready platform and related processes so universities can understand their critical services, dependencies, and recovery strategies.

This includes translating continuity and ITDR requirements into practical Salesforce-based workflows and data models, bringing together business, IT, and emergency management teams on a shared platform, and creating readiness metrics and dashboards that support decision-making before, during, and after disruptive events.
